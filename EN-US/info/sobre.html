Our History

Hello! I am aMathyzin – a PC optimization enthusiast with over 4 years of experience. My passion for technology led me to master various languages such as JavaScript, Python, C++ and C#, and to specialize in web design, video editing, programming, graphic design, as well as app and game development.

Currently, I am dedicated to creating innovative projects for my YouTube channel, also called aMathyzin. But aMathyzin has evolved—it has grown from being just one person into a project, a brand, and an organization. We have come together to develop the best computer optimization apps, ensuring satisfaction, superior performance, and most importantly, user safety – with most projects showing zero detections and all code available on our official GitHub.
