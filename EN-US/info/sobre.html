Our journey

A project born from the desire to optimise and share

Since 2020 aMathyzin has been publishing free content to speed up PCs, consoles and mobile devices. What started as tutorial videos evolved into a complete platform with scripts, packs and a dedicated support community.

Mission

Empower people with accessible tools so they can unlock the best performance from their devices with clear instructions and zero hidden costs.

Vision

Build a collaborative tech community that shares knowledge openly while keeping safety and transparency at the centre of every optimisation.

Timeline

Milestones that shaped aMathyzin into a reference for free performance tweaks.

2020 – First tutorials

YouTube videos covered essential Windows clean up steps and registry tweaks to free up performance quickly.

2021 – Discord community

The official server opened to centralise support, publish new scripts and gather real time feedback from members.

2022 – Collaborative packs

Special subscriber packs and community driven collections such as aMathyBoost were released with detailed documentation.

2023 onwards – Integrated platform

The project expanded to Xbox Cloud and mobile, with comprehensive guides and download pages for every optimisation.

What guides every decision

Principles that ensure quality and safety in each update.

Transparency

All files include release notes and a clear explanation of what is modified on your system.

Collaboration

The community helps review scripts, suggest improvements and provide peer support whenever you need it.

Accessibility

Free content in Portuguese and English with step-by-step videos for different knowledge levels.

Help shape what comes next

Share feedback, contribute code and follow new ideas on Discord and GitHub.
