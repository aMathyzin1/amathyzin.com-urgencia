RoBooster 2

Tool created by aMathyzin to apply advanced Roblox tweaks through Bloxstrap. It delivers stable sessions, reduces stuttering and automates the ideal configuration for each machine.

Quick overview

• Automatic quality and CPU priority adjustments.
• Ready-made profiles for different hardware.
• Easy rollback directly from Bloxstrap.

Essential steps

  • 1. Install Bloxstrap and sign in to your Roblox account.
  • 2. Make sure the .NET Desktop Runtime is up to date.
  • 3. Run RoBooster 2 as administrator and choose the recommended profile.
  • 4. Relaunch Roblox through Bloxstrap to apply the changes.

Support and recommendations

Create a restore point before applying deep changes. If you experience unexpected behaviour, revert the configuration through Bloxstrap or contact the team on Discord.
